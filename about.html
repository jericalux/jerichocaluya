My Journey

Ever since I was little I've only ever been engaged with things related to space, technology, and gadgets. I would build things, create different homemade inventions out of random crafts and supplies, and always wondered what else was out there. Eventually, that carried onto my educational career where majority of the classes I was taking was catered toward some sort of engineering path. That took a turn during my sophomore year however, that after much thought and hard decisions, it occurred to me how I was more interested in the innerworkings of business and how it's used and seen in our everyday lives. More specifically, the bridge between technology and business. Prior to my switch in majors, I would binge the show, Shark Tank, and involve myself more with different types of technology, computers, etc. I've always been a hands-on learner and love experiencing new things, and I feel like MIS was a perfect swtich for me. Since then, I've had the opportunity to work on various projects that have honed my skills in networking, database design, and programming and continue to seek opportunities for growth and learning.
